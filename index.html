Joust

What is Joust?
Joust is a package manager for emacs. Unlike every other emacs package manager, it doesn't suck.
Why "Joust"?
"Pacman" was taken.
Who are you?
Joust was written by Burke Libbey and Ryan Neufeld at Chromium 53.
Why is Joust awesome?
Joust makes it easy to manage and submit packages, and if you don't like hat's going on, you can make your own repository.
Sign me up already?
You can download joust at http://github.com/burke/joust.
